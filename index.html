Welcome to Brahmins Group

G.B. Shaw could not have been more honest when he said, “there is no
sincerer love than the love of food”.

Passionately in love with food and inspired everyday to create great-tasting,
nutritious products, the Brahmins Group has been enticing the world with
100% authentic, vegetarian culinary delights since 1987.
Close to three decades later, Brahmins' Vegetarian Promise continues to this
day, sharing the traditional taste of Kerala to all those who sincerely love
good food.
